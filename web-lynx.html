Web Lynx

Web Lynx is a creative concept that explores the connections between mathematics and the web. Just as a lynx is known for its sharp vision and agility, Web Lynx symbolizes the ability to navigate, link, and discover patterns across the vast landscape of mathematical ideas and digital networks.

Whether as a metaphor for mathematical exploration or as a project bridging math and technology, Web Lynx invites you to see mathematics as a living web of insights, connections, and creative possibilities.
